FAQ

Common questions about volunteering.

We want every volunteer to feel informed, welcomed, and ready to contribute.

Do I need prior experience?

No. We welcome volunteers with different backgrounds, skills, and levels of experience.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Some roles may be available online, while others are best suited for on-the-ground support.

How much time is required?

It depends on the role. Some activities are one-time, while others are ongoing and flexible.

Be the steady hand that helps a child feel seen.
