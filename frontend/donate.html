FAQ

Questions donors often ask.

We believe clarity builds trust. Here are answers to the most common concerns.

How do I know my donation reaches children?

We work with vetted local partners and maintain careful stewardship so donations are distributed with accountability.

Can I donate supplies instead of money?

Yes. Clothing, food, school supplies, and medical essentials are all welcome and directly distributed where needed.

Is my contribution tax-deductible?

Yes, donors can request documentation for eligible contributions through our support team.
